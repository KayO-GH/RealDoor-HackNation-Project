# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Renters preparing an affordable-housing application and housing navigators helping them. They need to turn a small set of documents into evidence a qualified human can review, without being screened, scored, or pressured into sharing more than is necessary.

## Product Purpose

RealDoor makes the path from supplied evidence to a renter-controlled readiness packet inspectable. It should reduce rework, preserve uncertainty, and make every material value, rule, correction, and packet outcome easy for a renter or navigator to verify.

## Brand Personality

Calm, evidence-first, empowering. The product feels like a trustworthy case file that belongs to the renter, not a black-box decision system.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a generic AI chatbot, a credit-score dashboard, a landlord revenue console, or a bank-style eligibility portal. Avoid visual signals that imply surveillance, ranking, or automatic approval.

## Design Principles

- Show evidence before conclusions.
- Make uncertainty actionable, never alarming.
- Keep the renter in control of every consequential handoff.
- Prefer a clear case-file workflow to AI theatrics.
- Make safety controls demonstrable, not merely stated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. The complete journey must work by keyboard, retain visible focus, communicate states without color alone, use readable citations and errors, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
